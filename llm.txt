# Zippily

> Zippily is an Auckland-based HubSpot Gold Partner and CRM implementation agency helping New Zealand businesses streamline their sales, marketing, and operations through HubSpot, Attio, and supporting B2B tech stack tools.

## About
Zippily is a trusted CRM partner based in Auckland, New Zealand. We specialise in affordable, fast, and people-first CRM implementation, training, and automation for B2B businesses. Our team works across HubSpot, Attio, Folk CRM, PandaDoc, and Aircall to deliver bespoke solutions tailored to each client's needs.

## Services
- CRM Implementation: Full scoping, configuration, and setup of HubSpot and Attio CRM portals
- HubSpot Training: Tailored training for teams from basic functions to advanced features
- Marketing Automations: Workflow setup, lead nurturing, and personalised customer journeys
- Buyer Journey Workshop: Mapping customer touchpoints from awareness to close
- CRM Audit: Identifying opportunities and ensuring clients get maximum value from their CRM
- Custom Integrations: Connecting HubSpot and Attio with wider tech stacks
- RevOps Retainer Packages: Ongoing revenue operations support and optimisation
